🛒 Retail Sales Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to uncover business insights, customer purchasing patterns, product performance, and revenue trends. Using data analytics techniques, the project transforms raw sales data into actionable insights that can support strategic business decisions.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze overall sales performance.

Identify top-performing products and categories.

Understand customer purchasing behavior.

Track revenue and profit trends.

Discover seasonal and regional sales patterns.

Provide data-driven recommendations for business growth.

📈 Key Insights

Identified top-performing products contributing significantly to overall revenue.

Discovered seasonal sales peaks and customer purchasing trends.

Highlighted regions generating the highest profits.

Detected underperforming products impacting profitability.

Provided recommendations for inventory optimization and revenue growth

💡 Business Recommendations

Increase focus on high-profit product categories.

Optimize inventory for top-selling products.

Implement targeted marketing campaigns for high-value customers.

Improve performance in low-profit regions through promotional strategies.

Reduce losses from underperforming products.
